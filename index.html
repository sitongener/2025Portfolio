Illustrator/Visual Designer

About

Sitong is an illustrator inspired by the intersection of myth and reality. Her work reimagines mythical creatures as symbols of identity, resilience, and transformation, creating modern narratives that invite viewers to find wonder in the everyday. In addition to traditional illustration, she uses 3D to compose narrative scenes and build interactive worlds, expanding the ways stories can be experienced. Sitong studied Illustration at the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), where she built a strong foundation in visual storytelling and conceptual thinking. Her practice blends technical skill with a love of narrative, encouraging audiences to rediscover magic in daily life.
